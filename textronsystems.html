Textron Systems

Software Engineer: August 2021-August 2022

My first rotation in the Textron Leadership Development Program (LDP) occured at Textron Systems in Hunt Valley, MD. There I primarily supported the Advanced Reconaissance Vehicle (ARV) software team, on the Vehicle Management System (VMS) effort. This role granted me my first professional software engineering experience and was a great way to kickstart my career in tech.

Below are notable involvements/contributions during my time with Systems:

  • Implemented Controller Area Network (CAN) bus interfacing applications and simulations for various hardware components using MOOS-IvP and Qt.
  • Designed user interface and infrastructure applications using Qt.
  • Wrote, executed, and documented results of target hardware testing using both VMS-related software as well as hardware in the ARV SIL as well as in the Alpha prototype vehicle during time in Textron Marine Land Systems (TMLS).
  • Exposed newly hired LDPs to the business, program, and their counterparts as a member of the LDP Social Media team specifically by maintaining the Class of 2022 LinkedIn page.
